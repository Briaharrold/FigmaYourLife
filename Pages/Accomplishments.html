Accomplishments

Corline Grew up… in Pontiac, Michigan. Coraline and her incredibly inattentive parents have recently moved all the way across the country. Living… in an apartment building called the Pink Palace in Ashland, Oregon. Coraline doesnt much like her new home. The creepy Pink Palace used to be an old mansion. Visiting… a place called the Other World. While exploring the Pink Palace, Coraline discovered a secret little door that leads to a parallel universe one that appears to be much better than her bland reality. Interests… exploring. Coraline loves a good adventure, and shes always wanted to go explore with her parents who, unlike her, happen to hate the outdoors. In the Other World, though, Coralines “Other Parents” pay very close attention to her.
